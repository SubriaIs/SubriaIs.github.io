Hi, I’m Subria, a passionate Full-Stack Software Engineer with experience in both front-end and back-end development. I am also experienced with Jenkins , Robot Framework , JAX-RS , J2EE , Hibernate and Spring Boot. I believe in building full-stack applications that prioritize user experience, integrating thoughtful design with strong engineering to create seamless, efficient solutions.


At the heart of my work are problem-solving, debugging, and testing. I approach each project from the user's perspective, aiming for clear and effective solutions. I believe testing and debugging are essential for delivering high-quality software. I also stay attentive to client requirements, ensuring the final product is not only functional and user-friendly but also aligned with their vision and goals.
